About Me

I am a Software Developer who has worked with a number of companies and startups. I'm passionate about Computer Vision technologies and Augmented Reality, so much so that I was willing to sit and write a 5+ page paper on it. It focused on using SLAM/RANSAC to generate 3D sparse point clouds from regular cameras. Then using machine learning to classify and simulate real objects in virtual space to occlude virtual objects in real-time. I've had the pleasure to present my work to my Honors Thesis board, US Naval Research (TEAR 3M), the 39th ASME IDETC/CIE conference, and anyone who is willing to sit for more than 10 minutes with me.

I also have experience in full-stack development, network security standards/procedures, and large-scale product development.
